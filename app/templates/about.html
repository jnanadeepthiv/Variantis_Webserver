Technical Overview

Variantis is a web-based tool developed using Flask, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. It integrates advanced libraries to deliver a seamless and interactive user experience for sequence analysis.

This project is a collaborative effort by Jnana Deepthi Vishnumolakala (Project Intern at IITH from SRM University, AP) and Dr. Gaurav Sharma from the SharmaG_omics Lab at IIT Hyderabad.

We extend our gratitude to the IIT Hyderabad and SRM University, Andhra Pradesh for their support and resources.
